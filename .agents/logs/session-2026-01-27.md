- Remote work normalized

### Insight: Vintage Creates Cohort Risk

If 10 companies founded in 2021-2022 all raised at similar valuations in similar conditions, they face:
- Simultaneous Series B pressure
- Shared macro sensitivity
- Correlated failure risk

**New Pattern**: "Vintage Cohort Risk"
- Companies of same vintage share environmental exposures
- Diversified portfolios should be vintage-diversified
- Vintage clustering indicates macro timing bets
