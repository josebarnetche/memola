# Follow-Up Email Sequences by Level

**Memola Medios S.A.S.**
**Version:** 1.0
**Updated:** Febrero 2026

---

## Sequence Types

1. **Post-Discovery** — After initial call, before proposal
2. **Post-Proposal** — After sending proposal
3. **Post-Delivery** — After project completion
4. **Nurture** — Long-term relationship maintenance

---

## Timing Guidelines

### Best Days to Send
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** Highest open rates
- **Avoid:** Monday AM, Friday PM, weekends

### Best Times
- **9:00-10:00:** Start of workday
- **14:00-15:00:** Post-lunch

### Response Windows
| Sequence | Expected Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| Post-Discovery | 24-48 horas |
| Post-Proposal | 3-7 días |
| Post-Delivery | 1-2 semanas |
| Nurture | 2-4 semanas |

---

## When to Stop

### Hard Stops
- 3 emails + 1 WhatsApp sin respuesta → Archive
- Client asks not to contact → Immediate stop
- Negative response → Acknowledge, stop

### Soft Stops
- "Not now" → Move to nurture (6 months)
- "Maybe later" → Calendar reminder (3 months)
- "Send info" but no engagement → One more follow-up, then archive
