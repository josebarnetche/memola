# MEMOLA Diagnostic Script (Internal Use)

Use this script to assess any new project.

---

## Section 1 — Reality Check
- What does the business sell?
- Who pays?
- What is the current bottleneck?

## Section 2 — Power & Control
- Who approves decisions?
- Who resists change?
- What cannot be touched?

## Section 3 — Assets
- Existing domains
- Existing content
- Existing data
- Existing reputation

## Section 4 — Constraints
- Budget ceiling
- Time availability
- Team capacity
- External dependencies

## Section 5 — MEMOLA Fit
- Is this strategic?
- Can value compound?
- Is MEMOLA additive here?

If 2 or more answers are negative, pause or decline.
