# MEMOLA Case Archetypes

This document defines abstract case types.
No client names. No vanity. Only patterns.

---

## Archetype 1 — Institution Modernization

### Context
- Traditional institutions
- High legitimacy, low digital presence
- Internal resistance to change

### MEMOLA Role
- Translator between tradition and modernity
- Builder of institutional narrative
- Silent operator

### Success Metric
Digital presence aligns with real-world authority.

---

## Archetype 2 — Artisanal to Scalable Brand

### Context
- Product quality is real
- Story exists but is fragmented
- Demand outpaces structure

### MEMOLA Role
- Systematize story
- Build e-commerce and ops logic
- Protect brand from dilution

### Success Metric
Growth without loss of identity.

---

## Archetype 3 — Cultural Event as Platform

### Context
- High emotional engagement
- Seasonal or episodic revenue
- Strong community attachment

### MEMOLA Role
- Extend lifecycle
- Monetize beyond tickets
- Build media assets

### Success Metric
Event becomes an ecosystem, not a date.

---

## Archetype 4 — Niche Vertical Platform

### Context
- Fragmented market
- High information asymmetry
- Clear professional users

### MEMOLA Role
- Build directory / aggregator
- Centralize demand
- Prepare for SaaS evolution

### Success Metric
Platform becomes reference point.
