## 2. NEA MARKET CHARACTERISTICS

### 2.1 Regional Economic Profile

The Nordeste Argentino (NEA) comprises four provinces: **Corrientes, Chaco, Misiones, and Formosa**. It is Argentina's second-most important cattle region after the Pampa plain.

| Province | Cattle Share | Economic Focus | MEMOLA Presence |
|----------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Corrientes** | 10% national (4.46M head) | Cattle breeding, wetlands, agriculture | ACTIVE - HQ in Mercedes |
| **Chaco** | ~4.5% national | Cattle, cotton, forestry | MONITORING |
| **Formosa** | ~4% national | Cattle, emerging agriculture | MONITORING |
| **Misiones** | ~1% national | Forestry, yerba mate, small cattle | MONITORING |

### 2.2 Critical Market Gap

> **19% of national cattle stock, but limited local processing capacity.**

This gap creates opportunities for:
- Regional frigorificos to reduce transport costs
- Local value-add capture currently lost to Buenos Aires/Santa Fe
- Marketing services that connect producers to buyers

## 7. CAPITALIZACION GANADERA & APARCERIA

### 7.1 Capitalizacion Ganadera (Livestock Capitalization)

**Definition:** One party provides cattle/capital, other provides land/care, both share production.

**Legal Status:** "Contrato innominado" - NOT regulated by Ley 13.246, full party autonomy.

| Operation Type | Landowner Share | Partner Share |
|----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Breeding (Cria)** | 45-50% calves | 50-55% calves |
| **Fattening (Invernada)** | 60-70% kg gained | 30-40% kg gained |

### 7.2 Aparceria Pecuaria (Livestock Sharecropping)

| Structure | Best For | Key Feature |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Aparceria Pecuaria** | Regulated partnerships | 50/50 default, 3-year minimum |
| **Fideicomiso Ganadero** | Multiple investors | Separated patrimony, professional admin |
| **Arrendamiento con Mejoras** | Lease-based | Reduced rent for improvements |

### 7.3 Returns Expected

| Operation | Annual Return |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Breeding (30 months)** | 15-18% |
| **Fattening** | 10-14% |
| **Mixed** | 12-16% |

### 7.4 MEMOLA Relevance

Understanding these structures allows MEMOLA to:
1. Advise clients on partnership marketing
2. Position services for both landowners and investors
3. Identify capital flows for business development opportunities
