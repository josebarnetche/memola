## 2. NEA MARKET CHARACTERISTICS

### Regional Economic Profile

The Nordeste Argentino (NEA) comprises four provinces: **Corrientes, Chaco, Misiones, and Formosa**. It is Argentina's second-most important cattle region after the Pampa plain.

| Province | Cattle Share | Economic Focus |
|----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Corrientes** | 10% national (4.46M head) | Cattle breeding, wetlands, agriculture |
| **Chaco** | ~4.5% national | Cattle, cotton, forestry |
| **Formosa** | ~4% national | Cattle, emerging agriculture |
| **Misiones** | ~1% national | Forestry, yerba mate, small cattle |

### Critical Market Gap

> **19% of national cattle stock, but limited local processing capacity.**

This gap creates opportunities for:
- Regional frigorificos to reduce transport costs
- Local value-add capture currently lost to Buenos Aires/Santa Fe
- Marketing services that connect producers to buyers

### Economic Activity Patterns

| Sector | Activity Share | Trend |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| **Agropecuario** | 35% | Stable growth |
| **Comercio** | 25% | Consolidation |
| **Industria** | 15% | Emerging |
| **Servicios/Otros** | 15% | Formalizing |
| **Tecnología** | 5% | VACUUM — near-zero tech company presence |

## 3. KEY SECTORS

### Cattle / Livestock (Ganaderia)

**The dominant sector. Primary focus for MEMOLA services.**

| Segment | Characteristics | Service Fit |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Criadores** (Breeders) | Extensive grazing, traditional, family-owned | Remate marketing, branding |
| **Invernadores** (Fatteners) | Pasture-based finishing, some feedlots | Performance marketing, export positioning |
| **Cabañas** (Breeding stock) | Genetics, high-value auctions | Premium positioning, event coverage |

**Key Data Points:**
- Average carrying capacity (malezal): 0.45-0.55 EV/ha
- Meat production (natural pasture): 70-80 kg/ha/year
- Potential with improved pastures: 200-300 kg/ha/year (+3-4x)

### Consignatarias (Livestock Commission Houses)

**Network nodes that control cattle flow and auction access.**

| Role | Function | MEMOLA Opportunity |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Intermediary | Connect producers to buyers | Access to auction calendar |
| Finance | Provide credit to producers | Payment cycle intelligence |
| Marketing | Promote remates | Demand for digital marketing |

**Why They Matter:** Consignatarias are not just clients—they are **network infrastructure**. Each one provides market intelligence and industry access.

### Frigorificos (Processing Plants)

| **Chaco** | 26 |

| **Misiones** | 18 |
| **Formosa** | 6 |

**Opportunity:**

## 6. CAPITALIZACIÓN GANADERA & APARCERÍA

### Capitalizacion Ganadera (Livestock Capitalization)

**Definition:** One party provides cattle/capital, other provides land/care, both share production.

**Legal Status:** "Contrato innominado" - NOT regulated by Ley 13.246, full party autonomy.

| Operation Type | Typical Landowner Share | Typical Partner Share |
|----------------|-------------------------|----------------------|
| **Breeding (Cria)** | 45-50% calves | 50-55% calves |
| **Fattening (Invernada)** | 60-70% kg gained | 30-40% kg gained |

### Partnership Structures

| Structure | Best For |
|-----------|----------|
| **Aparceria Pecuaria** | Regulated partnerships |
| **Fideicomiso Ganadero** | Multiple investors |
| **Arrendamiento con Mejoras** | Lease-based with improvements |

### Expected Returns

| Operation | Annual Return |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Breeding (30 months)** | 15-18% |
| **Fattening** | 10-14% |
| **Mixed** | 12-16% |
